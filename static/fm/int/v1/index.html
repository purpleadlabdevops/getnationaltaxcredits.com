How One Business Owner Received $1.4 Million For Keeping His Doors Open Throughout 2020 and 2021

Other eateries closed their doors, now his is thriving

Owning a restaurant is tough when the economy is good. And when it’s rough, like the lockdowns of 2020 and 2021, it’s nearly impossible!

But one brave restaurant owner kept every last one of his employees on the payroll throughout the lockdowns. Now he’s coming up huge! He took care of them, now the federal government is taking care of him.

There is a program known as Employee Retention Credit (ERC) which was designed to reward companies that were impacted by the pandemic. If they kept their employees on the payroll during the pandemic, they’re eligible for up to $26,000 per W2 employee for 2020 and 2021.

For brave business owners like this restaurateur, it’s a way to say thank you for doing the right thing. And even though he received PPP loans, he was STILL ELIGIBLE for these ERC credits.

Any company in any industry that wants to learn more can take a 60-second quiz to see if they’re eligible for these ERC credits.
